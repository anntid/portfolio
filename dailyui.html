Daily UI

Alongside my studies I have created UI designs from the Daily UI prompts. It has allowed me to have fun with my learnings and be creative while still following a brief. At the start I only created one version of my design, but after having completed a number of prompts I started making both phone and desktop versions of my designs to practice both of the two mediums.


I strive to create designs that are both easy to navigate and easy on the eyes. In my work featured below I have included both simplicity and a fun visual look. To me, the simplicity and ease of navigation is amplified even more when paired with appealing and fun visuals.
