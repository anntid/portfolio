About

Hej! My name is Annika, and I’m a Stockholm based designer.


I am currently studying Digital Media Creative at Hyper Island and my main interest is UI design. Using digital solutions should be an enjoyable experience, and I want to contribute to that by creating designs that are easy to navigate and understand.


During my studies I have also found a passion for web development, and have learned to code. I enjoy the logical and rule-bound aspect of coding, as well as seeing my ideas come to life through my own doing!


In the many group projects I have been part of through my studies, I have been described as having a calming effect on my teammates. I am a planner by nature, so by structuring my own work, I also influence my teammates and help structure the work we do together. I am a doer and thanks to my need for structure, I can perform well under pressure.


Apart from design, I enjoy reading, cooking, video games and caring for my plants!


I look forward to hearing from you! :)
