During spring of 2021, I completed the Responsive Web Design certificate from freeCodeCamp to learn about web design and development.


It was in a coding project during my studies that I discovered my interest for coding. The logical and rule-bound creativity stood out to me, and so I decided to enroll in an additional course outside of school to learn more.


Whilst challenging, it was a fun experience! I really enjoyed learning something new, seeing it come to life and even be able to interact with my websites after I had completed them.


I decided to design and code my own portfolio to put my knowledge to the test, and the product is this website you're on right now.
